# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Project Overwatch ("POW", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Roblox Account Information**: Username, user ID, and avatar (obtained via OAuth)
- **Discord Account Information**: Username, user ID, and avatar (obtained via OAuth)
- **Service Usage Data**: Shift times, LOA requests, punishment records, and moderation actions

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Game Logs**: Player join/leave logs, kill logs, and command logs from ERLC servers (via PRC API)
- **Usage Analytics**: Basic usage data through PostHog for service improvement
- **Security Logs**: IP addresses and access patterns for security purposes

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the Service
- Track staff shifts and quota compliance
- Record and manage player punishments
- Enable moderation and automation features
- Send notifications via Discord
- Improve and optimize the Service
- Ensure security and prevent abuse

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Server Administrators**: Admins can view staff data within their servers
- **Third-Party Services**: As necessary to provide the Service (Clerk, Discord, Roblox, PRC API)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored in secure databases
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
- Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel and server administrators

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Data**: Retained while you are an active member of a server
- **Shift/Punishment Records**: Retained for server record-keeping purposes
- **Deleted Accounts**: We may retain anonymized data for analytics
- **Investigation Messages**: Auto-deleted after 3 minutes per GDPR compliance

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (subject to server administrator approval and record-keeping requirements)
- **Portability**: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact your server administrator.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## 9. International Users

By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your residence, which may have different data protection rules.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience.

- **Essential Cookies**: Required for authentication (Clerk) and security. These cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics Cookies**: Used by PostHog to understand service usage.
    - If you **Accept** cookies: We store a persistent cookie to analyze your usage trends over time.
    - If you **Decline** cookies: We use a **Cookieless Memory Mode**. No data is stored on your device. Analytics data is reset every time you refresh the page.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via the dashboard footer.

In compliance with Roblox OAuth requirements:
